Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we deliver outside Sweden. Please contact us for the shipping price, as it needs to be calculated based on your location and our logistics.

Orders over $50 qualify for free shipping. For orders under this amount, a shipping fee will be added, visible in your shopping bag at checkout.

Yes, you can return products. However, please note that this does not apply to our Pre-Loved items. We offer insurance against transit damage for these items.

You can find our contact information on our Contacts page.

If you are on Gotland, you can visit us at Sanda Nystugu 573, 623 79 Klintehamn, Gotland. Otherwise, you can reach us by flight or by ferry via Destination Gotland.

Our product range is diverse, but it primarily includes items that we personally love and use. Our philosophy centers around offering products that resonate with our own tastes and preferences.

Pre-Loved items are previously owned products that have found their way to us. We resell these items in our shop because we adore them but cannot use them ourselves.

We prioritize our visiting customers and locals by offering them the first choice. This approach adds value and enhances the customer experience. However, if you spot an item you like on our social media, we can certainly arrange for you to purchase it. Nothing is impossible!

We are not currently offering custom malas, but this is something we plan to do in the future. Please feel free to email us with your requirements, and we will see what we can do. As always, we believe nothing is impossible!
